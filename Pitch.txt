1. Game idea: You are playing as a bomb and the game is very similar to pacman.

2. Scenario and characters: There are plenty of "coins" everywhere around
		            the map with enemies walking around.

3. Goal: The characters goal is to accumulate the amount of points before the
	 timer ends.

4. Win: Gain the amount of Points before the timer ends. Lose: Get caught
	by the enemy or not gaining the amount of points in the time given.

5. Progression levels: At a certain amount of points, the enemies will be faster
		       to make it more challenging.

6. PacMan

7. Skills or interests: Someone who is comfortable with making a timer, working
			with the right size of images to make the world look great!
